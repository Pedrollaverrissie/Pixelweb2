Mysteriously named as Pixels.. is one of the most famous black-hat hackers turned ethical hackers in history and is considered by many to be the no 1 hacker in the world. In September 2023, Pixels enrolled at Chuka University in Kenya, then went abroad for further studies on software engineering at Oxford University in United kingdom. He insisted on applying only to Reed, although it was an expensive school that his parents could ill afford. Pixels soon befriended Petrokarl,who was Oxford's university student body president at that time.

Pixels was born on July 17, 2005, in Kilifi, Kenya, but grew up in Mombasa and Nairobi Kenya. He attended Kanga High School in Migori, Kenya, before pursuing further education. Pixels holds a Bachelor of Electrical Engineering (BEENG) degree from the Chuka University, Kenya. He later earned a Master of Software engineering (EENG) from the Oxford University in UK. Pixels further pursued his studies at the University of Ghent in Belgium, where he obtained a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) degree in engineering.He holds honorary Doctorate degrees from the University of Cape Coast in Ghana and Bells University in Nigeria. He is an CEO of the KPLC of Kenya and Tanganyika. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Certified KPLC Secretaries of Kenya , a Fellow of the Kenya Institute of Management (FKIM) and Honorary Fellow of the African Academy of Sciences (FAAS).
